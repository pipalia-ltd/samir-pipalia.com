SAP Business One eCommerce case study

Shopify to SAP Business One integration for B2B order processing

A multi-country SAP Business One environment needed B2B eCommerce orders to move into ERP reliably, with less manual effort and a clearer support model for operational teams.

Problem

Shopify orders needed dependable flow into SAP Business One without creating fragile manual workarounds or support confusion.

Solution

Delivered and supported Shopify to SAP Business One integration for automated B2B order processing and operational follow-through.

Outcome

Reduced manual friction, improved eCommerce order handling and gave support teams a clearer path for issue ownership.

Context

eCommerce integration has to preserve both customer experience and ERP integrity. Orders, customer references, pricing, fulfilment expectations and error handling all need to be understood as one business process rather than isolated technical tasks.

What Samir owned

  • Delivery and support of B2B Shopify order flow into SAP Business One.
  • ERP-side analysis of order processing, operational impact and support requirements.
  • Collaboration with business teams to make integration behaviour understandable and maintainable.
  • Support maturity around eCommerce and ERP incidents after delivery.
