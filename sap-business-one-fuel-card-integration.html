SAP Business One integration case study

SAP Business One fuel card integration for BP and Total acceptance

Downstream retail operations needed fuel card processing connected into SAP Business One and the wider integration landscape. The work required ERP understanding, integration accountability and coordination across external vendors and internal teams.

Problem

Fuel card acceptance needed to work reliably with SAP Business One and surrounding systems, without leaving operational gaps between vendors.

Solution

Managed delivery across SAP Business One, MuleSoft collaboration, BPA, SQL and vendor teams, translating requirements into executable integration work.

Outcome

Enabled coordinated fuel card processing through an ERP and integration programme with clearer ownership and delivery control.

Context

Fuel card integration is not only a technical data-flow problem. It touches retail operations, finance, reconciliation, external providers, ERP master data and operational support. Delivery needed careful coordination between SAP Business One, integration middleware and business stakeholders.

What Samir owned

  • Project management and technical coordination across SAP Business One and integration teams.
  • Requirement clarification between business users, vendors and developers.
  • ERP-side thinking around data, process impact, supportability and exception handling.
  • Delivery accountability across SAP Business One, SQL, BPA / TaskCentre and MuleSoft touchpoints.
