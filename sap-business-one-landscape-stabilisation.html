SAP Business One case study

SAP Business One landscape stabilisation across 38 companies

A complex oil and gas ERP estate needed stronger ownership across SAP Business One, integrations, reporting, automation and support. The work centred on stabilising a commercially exposed application landscape while keeping business operations moving.

Problem

Multiple SAP Business One companies, geographies and operational teams needed consistent issue ownership and technical direction.

Solution

Provided L3 ownership across SAP Business One, Boyum, Crystal Reports, BPA automation and related integration flows.

Outcome

Improved stability, escalation quality and cross-team accountability in a high-risk ERP environment.

Context

The landscape supported oil and gas operations across a broad SAP Business One estate. The environment included multiple companies, complex reporting requirements, business-critical integrations and a mix of vendor, internal and operational stakeholders.

What Samir owned

  • L3 escalation for SAP Business One incidents and complex process issues.
  • Coordination between ERP users, technical teams, vendors and business stakeholders.
  • Investigation across SAP Business One, Boyum, Crystal Reports, BPA / TaskCentre and integration dependencies.
  • Practical stabilisation work focused on reliability, clarity and repeatable support paths.
