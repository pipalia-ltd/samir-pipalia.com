Tiny Wins Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Tiny Wins is a simple local-first app for logging small daily wins, calm moments, and health stats such as weight, sleep, blood pressure, and blood sugar.

Data You Enter

Tiny Wins lets you enter:

  • daily wins and categories
  • calm or thought logs
  • health stats, including weight, sleep, blood pressure, and blood sugar
  • timestamps for logged entries

How Data Is Stored

In the current version, this data is stored locally on your device using local app storage.

Tiny Wins does not currently create an account, upload your entries to a server, sync data to the cloud, or share your entries with third parties.

Health Information

Tiny Wins may store health-related information that you choose to enter. The app is intended for personal tracking and reflection only. It is not a medical device, does not diagnose or treat medical conditions, and should not replace professional medical advice.

Third Parties

The current version does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Data Deletion

You can remove individual entries inside the app by long-pressing an entry and choosing remove. You can remove all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage on your Android device.

Changes

This policy may be updated when Tiny Wins adds new features such as cloud backup, accounts, analytics, or crash reporting.
